About Redhawk Ventures

Redhawk Ventures is a student-run venture capital fund that invests in Miami students and alumni. We are a cross-disciplinary fund, and generally invest $25,000 per company. Redhawk Ventures also works with the local startup ecosystem in and around Cincinnati, including funds such as Refinery Ventures, CincyTech, and Centrifuse, along with startup accelerator programs like Ocean Accelerator.

Redhawk Ventures is currently recruiting for the Fall 2020 semester. We welcome both upperclassmen and underclassmen of all majors and studies to apply. Please use this google form to submit an application.
