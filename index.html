Redhawk Ventures

Redhawk Ventures is committed to connecting Miami University to the complex and diverse world of the entrepreneurial ecosystem, and venture capital. The purpose of this blog is to provide an educational resource for students at Miami to learn about how their fields of study connect to startups.

There is a common misconception that venture capital is a strictly financial field, but this is not the case. Venture as a field is focused on humans first and finances second. Startups come from all fields of study, and therefore so do the individuals in venture capital that strive to learn about and support these startups.
